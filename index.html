My Programmes

STEP UP

( Elevate and enhance online teaching possibilities )


An online enhancement programme for academicians and educators to inculcate online teaching skills in them. It also includes classes regarding the values that one should uphold while taking classes online thereby keeping in mind the emotional and psychological state of students

CONNECT

( Common orientation for new-age competency training )


Connect is an induction programme for first year students to acquire required student skills. This will help them to build the needed mindset that a student should possess to cope up with their new learning environment and thereby adapt to it confidently and productively

MAP

( Magnify placement possibilities for job-seeking individuals )


MAP is a placement enhancement programme for the job-seeking youngsters to help them acquire necessary interview skills and personality vibe to ensure their placement in the desired job.

EXAMINENCE

( Examination eminence enhancement program )


This program is designed to help students face their examination confidently. This is an overall examination guide to calm their minds, Also to boost their memory to help them give their best in exams without being afraid of their results.

BEEP

( Basic English Enhancement Programme )


Programme to improve proficiency in the English language and thereby upgrading the ability to communicate effectively in English.

D-SMART

Session to enhance attitude and aptitude in online presentation/teaching/meeting. This session helps individuals to transact their sessions effectively

POWER OFF

Screen/Social Media/Digital detoxification programme. It helps individuals to balance their usage of digital platforms thereby helping them to create a better version of themselves.
